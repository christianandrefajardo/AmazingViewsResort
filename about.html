Amazing View is a beautiful mountain resort situated at the foot of the mountains of Rizal. Its scenic view of the Sierra Madre mountains and the agricultural landscape of the Mabitac Valley lets you escape from the bustling city life.

Together with the picturesque scenery, our amazing facilities are perfect for your team building and family outing activities.

It is just 10 minutes away from the latest tourist attraction near Manila, the new Pililla Windmill.
